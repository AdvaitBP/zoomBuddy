About Zoom Engagement Analyzer

Zoom Engagement Analyzer is designed to help educators quickly assess student participation during Zoom sessions. The tool processes Zoom transcripts and chat logs, aggregates engagement metrics by student based on a provided roster, and computes category counts and lesson relevancy scores.

Built with Flask and Bootstrap, this web application offers a simple, intuitive interface to upload files and view results. The backend leverages natural language processing techniques—including stemming and semantic similarity—to deliver insightful metrics.

Whether you're looking to understand which students are most engaged or identify areas for improvement in class discussions, this tool provides a robust solution.
